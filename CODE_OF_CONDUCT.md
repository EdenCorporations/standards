# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

EdenCORP is building technology that matters. Doing that well requires a team where every person — regardless of background, experience level, or role — can contribute with confidence and without fear.

We are committed to maintaining a professional, respectful, and inclusive environment for all contributors, employees, contractors, and collaborators.

---

## Expected Behaviour

All participants in EdenCORP engineering spaces (code reviews, issue trackers, Slack, meetings, documentation) are expected to:

- **Be professional and direct.** State your position clearly. Disagree with ideas, not people.
- **Assume good intent.** Most miscommunications stem from ambiguity, not malice.
- **Give constructive feedback.** Critique code and systems — not the person who wrote them.
- **Respect others' time.** Keep discussions focused and actionable.
- **Acknowledge expertise.** Recognise that others may have context you lack. Ask before you assume.
- **Support learning.** Be patient with colleagues who are developing skills. Share knowledge generously.
- **Maintain confidentiality.** Do not share internal discussions, code, or strategic information outside authorised channels.

---

## Unacceptable Behaviour

The following will not be tolerated under any circumstances:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation based on race, gender, sexual orientation, disability, nationality, religion, or any other characteristic.
- Personal attacks, insults, or dismissive language in code reviews, issues, or any EdenCORP communication channel.
- Deliberate exclusion of contributors from discussions relevant to their work.
- Sharing confidential information without authorisation.
- Intentional sabotage of codebases, systems, or processes.
- Misrepresenting the work of others.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all EdenCORP-controlled spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories and issue trackers
- Internal Slack workspaces and channels
- Video calls and in-person meetings
- Any communication made in an official EdenCORP capacity

---

## Reporting

If you witness or experience behaviour that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. Report it directly to your team lead or `conduct@edencorp.ai`.
2. Reports are handled confidentially.
3. Retaliation against reporters is a separate violation and will be treated with equal seriousness.

---

## Enforcement

Violations are assessed case by case. Outcomes may include:

- A formal warning.
- Temporary suspension from EdenCORP repositories and communication channels.
- Permanent removal from EdenCORP projects.
- Escalation to HR or legal teams where appropriate.

Decisions made by `@edencorp/leadership` are final.
